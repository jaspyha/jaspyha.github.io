About Me

I'm pursuing a 4+1 in Computer Engineering (BS) and Intelligent Systems, Robotics, and Control (MS) at UCSD. I find marine robotics to be a very impactful and challenging developing field. Previously, I worked as a Summer Research Fellow in WARPLab (WHOI's Autonomous Robotics and Perception Laboratory) at Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution. My project was on Visual Fish Detection and Tracking in Coral Reefs.

Whenever I'm not working on robots or computers, you can find me just about anywhere!
Sometimes it'll be out surfing, fishing, practicing Wushu, dancing or exploring the California coastline and forests.
I'm always up to push myself mentally or physically and learn new things.
My current challenge is running a sub 4-hour at my first ever Marathon in San Diego!
As of now, I've immersed myself in Hip-Hop/Breaking, Afro-Cuban, Salsa/Bachata, and Contact improvisation dances.
